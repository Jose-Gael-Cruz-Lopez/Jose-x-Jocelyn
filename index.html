Who We Are

We map the pipeline from first internship to first offer.

No one should have to guess their way through recruiting. We are two first-gen students in tech who believe every step — from discovery to onboarding — should be visible, mapped, and community-supported.

Jose covers getting in. Jocelyn covers staying and growing. Together, we form the relay that career centers never built.

The On-the-Ground Scout

First-gen computer engineering student actively recruiting, sharing in real time what works and what fails. ColorStack Fellow. Hackathon winner. AI/ML Fellow. Soft robotics researcher.

  • Internship and early-career role discovery
  • Application strategy and resume reviews
  • Coffee chats and peer mentorship
  • Real-time recruiting insights
⚡ Student Perspective Getting In

The Bridge Builder

First-gen Latina in tech working in data at Fidelity. Microsoft LEAD Marketing Lead. Student ambassador. She crossed the bridge from student to industry and translates the mystery of early-career life into transparent guides.

  • Converting internships into return offers
  • Navigating compensation and negotiations
  • Early-career growth and office navigation
  • Onboarding and first-90-day strategies
🎯 Post-Grad Perspective Staying & Growing

The Relay Model

Live notes from both sides of the bridge. Jose documents the journey as it happens. Jocelyn documents what life and growth look like on the other side. This handoff — from undergraduate recruiting to post-grad full-time reality — is what students never get from campus career centers.

  • Student-to-early-career pipeline, mapped end to end
  • Split-screen perspectives on every topic
  • Community-supported recruiting sprints
  • For first-gen and underrepresented talent in tech and data

What We Offer

Programs & Content

01

LinkedIn Series

One Journey, Two Perspectives

Recurring co-hosted LinkedIn series: one role analyzed from both the student and post-grad lens. Inbox-to-offer case studies, split-screen carousels, and "what we wish we knew" frameworks.

02

Sprint Cohorts

Last-Minute Internship Sprint

Small 20–30 person sprints to move through a specific career milestone. Weekly application blocks, accountability sessions, interview prep, and storytelling workshops.

03

Offer-to-Onboarding

Bridge Year Sprint

Workshops on reading benefits, setting first-90-day goals, building workplace relationships. For students between graduation and their first role — project building, networking, and mental health.

04

Opportunity Board

Curated + Accountability

Public, curated board of late-breaking internships and early-career roles. Layered with accountability threads — commit to applying, share when you hear back.

05

Templates & Scripts

Outreach Toolkit

Ready-to-use outreach messages, recruiter follow-ups, manager one-on-one templates, and email scripts for asking for feedback. Copy, customize, send.
