Jose x Jocelyn maps the pipeline from first internship to first offer for first-gen and underrepresented students in tech. We combine real-time student perspective with early-career professional insight, creating a relay that campus career centers never built.

Our approach is simple: Jose covers getting in — discovering internships, early-ID programs, and application strategy as an actively recruiting student. Jocelyn covers staying and growing — converting internships to offers, negotiating compensation, and navigating early-career growth at a major firm.

No one should have to guess their way from first internship search to first offer. Every step should be visible, mapped, and community-supported. We are the visible relay — live notes from both sides of the bridge — that turns confusion into clarity through concrete next steps.
