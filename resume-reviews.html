Section 02 · How It Works

How the Resume Library Works

Community-sourced. Outcome-verified. Completely free.

01
Browse real submissions

Every resume in this library was submitted by a real student or early-career candidate who used it to get a response. Filter by role, company, background, and stage to find examples that match where you are in the process.

02
Learn what works

Seeing real resumes is more useful than a template. You can look at formatting, bullet structure, experience framing, and project descriptions from people who were recently in your exact position.

03
Submit yours

If your resume helped you land an interview, an offer, or a callback, you can contribute it to the library so others can learn from it. You control your name visibility and whether downloads are allowed.
