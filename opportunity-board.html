Section 02

Where these opportunities come from

Not scraped, not auto-filled. Every listing starts with a human decision.

The board is not scraped from the internet or auto-filled from job APIs. Every listing begins as a role Jose or Jocelyn has found, vetted, and shared — usually on LinkedIn — or as a program surfaced on the Bridge Year Hub. That includes tech apprenticeships like Pinterest, Google, and Microsoft LEAP, early-career rotational programs like Salesforce Futureforce, and niche fellowships or research roles that are especially strong for first-gen and underrepresented students.

When you see something here, you know it has already passed a basic "would we send this to our own friends?" filter. We don't post roles we wouldn't apply to ourselves or recommend to a first-gen student still figuring things out.
